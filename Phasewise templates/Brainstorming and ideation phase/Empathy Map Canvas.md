## What is an Empathy Map?

An empathy map is a simple, easy-to-digest visual that captures knowledge about a user's behaviours and attitudes. It is a useful tool to help teams better understand their users. Creating an effective solution requires understanding the true problem and the person who is experiencing it.

## User Persona 1: The Busy Patient (Primary User)

**Name:** Priya S. | **Age:** 32 | **Occupation:** Software professional | **Location:** Hyderabad

### 🧠 THINK & FEEL
> *What really counts? Major preoccupations, worries & aspirations*

- "Will I be able to find a doctor who specializes in what I need?"
- "I'm worried my symptoms might be serious — I need an appointment urgently."
- "Why is it so hard to know if a doctor is trustworthy or available?"
- "I just want a clear, quick way to see doctors and their fees before committing."
- "I hope my medical documents are kept private and secure."
- "I wish I could get a reminder so I don't miss my appointment."

### 👂 HEAR
> *What friends say, what influencers say, what the environment conveys*

- "My friend told me she found a good cardiologist on a healthcare app — maybe I should try."
- "People say digital health platforms are not reliable for serious conditions."
- "My colleague used an app and got an appointment in 5 minutes."
- "News: telemedicine is growing, but rural access remains a challenge."
- "My doctor said I should get follow-up done within a week."

### 👁 SEE
> *Environment, friends, what the market offers*

- Long queues at hospitals and clinics
- Friend using Practo or similar apps
- Ads for telemedicine platforms on social media
- Doctors unavailable on short notice
- Physical receipts, handwritten appointment cards
- Confusing multi-step hospital websites

### 🗣 SAY & DO
> *Attitude in public, appearance, behavior towards others*

- Googles "best cardiologist near me"
- Asks friends for doctor recommendations on WhatsApp
- Calls hospital front desk — gets placed on hold for 20 minutes
- Eventually books an appointment through a known contact
- "I ended up paying ₹500 consultation fee just to be referred elsewhere"
- Takes a half-day off from work to visit a hospital unnecessarily

### 😣 PAIN
> *Fears, frustrations, obstacles*

- Cannot easily compare doctors by specialization, fees, and availability
- Uncertainty about doctor credentials and authenticity
- No real-time appointment tracking — is it confirmed or not?
- Takes too many steps to book even a simple general consultation
- Medical documents shared via unencrypted email or WhatsApp
- Fear of sharing personal health data on unknown platforms

### 🎯 GAIN
> *Wants/needs, measures of success*

- One platform to search, compare, and book verified doctors
- Instant confirmation and status updates for appointments
- Ability to upload and share medical records securely per appointment
- Transparent fee display and doctor ratings before booking
- Notification alerts when doctor approves or rejects the appointment
- Easy cancellation if plans change

## User Persona 2: The Doctor (Secondary User)

**Name:** Dr. Ramesh K. | **Age:** 42 | **Specialization:** Orthopedic Surgeon | **Location:** Bangalore

### 🧠 THINK & FEEL
- "I'm losing potential patients because I have no digital presence."
- "Managing appointments via phone is inefficient — I need a system."
- "I want patients to see my qualifications and experience before booking."
- "Will the platform vet patients to avoid no-shows?"

### 👂 HEAR
- "Other doctors in my hospital use digital platforms and see more patients."
- "Patients complain they can never reach the hospital to reschedule."

### 👁 SEE
- Competitor doctors building digital profiles and attracting online patients
- Clinic receptionists overwhelmed with manual scheduling
- Appointment no-shows causing revenue loss

### 🗣 SAY & DO
- Asks clinic manager to maintain a Google Sheet for appointments
- Manually confirms patient appointments via phone call
- Refers patients to WhatsApp groups for document sharing
- "I wish I could just see my upcoming appointments in one place."

### 😣 PAIN
- No centralized tool to manage availability timings
- Cannot view patient-uploaded medical documents ahead of consultation
- Appointment no-shows with no advance cancellation notice
- Manual credential verification reduces trust with new patients

### 🎯 GAIN
- A verified digital profile with specialization, fee, and availability
- Centralized appointment dashboard to approve/reject/complete consultations
- Pre-uploaded patient medical documents for informed consultation
- Email notifications for new booking requests

---

## User Persona 3: The Platform Admin (Tertiary User)

**Name:** Admin | **Role:** Platform Governance & Operations

### 😣 PAIN
- Cannot easily track fraudulent doctor listings
- No centralized view of all users, doctors, and appointments
- No audit trail for appointment status changes

### 🎯 GAIN
- Full admin dashboard with user, doctor, and appointment management
- Doctor verification workflow before going live on platform
- System health monitoring and activity logs
